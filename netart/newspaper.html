Weekly News

AFTER MONTHS OF RAIN, MOST MAY NOT CREDIT ACADEMIA.

After many months of non-stop rain fall, the rain has finally stopped. Though, many areas have been flooded, drowning several people, and injuring many more. More than half of the residents of this area have been displaced from their homes unable to go back as buildings and the surrounding areas are still flooded.
